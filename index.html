CINEMA DESERVES A SECOND LIFE

Nouveau Roadshow reinvents theatrical-first distribution for bold, genre-based films — horror, action, sci-fi, and dark comedy — prioritizing underrepresented creators, underserved audiences, and independent cinemas.

We deliver curated film slates, revenue-sharing deals, bundled marketing materials, and a touring model inspired by punk bands and roadshow legends.

"NR helped us turn a profit and find our audience when no one else would take a chance on our vision."

— Maria G., Filmmaker
